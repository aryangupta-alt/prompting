# Brand Rules

The following rules are used when selecting the best advertisement variant.

## Rule 1: No Superlatives

Avoid exaggerated claims such as:

* Best
* Cheapest
* Number One
* Perfect
* Greatest

## Rule 2: Professional Tone

Advertising copy should maintain a professional and trustworthy tone.

## Rule 3: No Feature Hallucination

Do not mention features that are not present in the extracted product attributes.

## Rule 4: Highlight Key Benefits

Focus on the actual product benefits and features relevant to the target audience.

## Rule 5: Keep Messaging Concise

The advertisement should be clear, concise, and easy to understand.
