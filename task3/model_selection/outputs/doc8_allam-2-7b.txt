Bullet points:

1. Blockchain is a distributed digital ledger technology for secure and transparent transaction recording, used by cryptocurrencies but also applied in various fields like supply chain management, healthcare, and finance.
2. Blockchain offers benefits such as transparency and security; however, scalability remains a challenge to be addressed.
3. Blockchain underpins the underlying transactions and operations of cryptocurrencies, but its applications reach far beyond digital currencies, touching sectors like supply chain management, healthcare, and finance where it promises enhanced transparency, security, and performance.
