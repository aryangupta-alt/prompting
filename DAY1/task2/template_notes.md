# Why Each Prompt Section Matters

## Role
Defines the expertise and perspective of the AI.
Without it, responses may be generic.

## Context
Provides background information about the problem.
Without it, the model may misunderstand the purpose of the task.

## Task
Clearly specifies what needs to be done.
Without it, outputs may be unfocused.

## Constraints
Sets rules and limitations.
Without constraints, responses may become too long or include unwanted information.

## Format
Ensures consistency in output structure.
Without it, responses may vary significantly.

## Examples
Demonstrate the expected behavior.
Without examples, the model may interpret the task differently and produce less accurate results.
